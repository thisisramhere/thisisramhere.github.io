About Me

As the CEO of Machbee Innovations Pvt Ltd and Managing Director of Machbee Future Appliances Pvt Ltd, Rahul Mohan is responsible for running all facets of the business. Rahul has a proven executive management track record and over 5 years of experience driving research and technological advancement in multiple sectors like Artificial Intelligence, Telecommunication, ConsumerTech, HealthTech and Aviation. Additionally, experienced in large-scale production management from product design to bulk manufacturing, including the complete life cycle of multiple products, he is also well-versed in data analytics, machine learning and digital media.

Rahul received Special Jury Award for the Best Innovative Project at APJ Abdul Kalam Innovation Awards held at Adi Shankara Engineering College, Kalady In 2016. Rahul also holds multiple patents, both Indian and international across various fields. In addition to these patents Rahul has contributed to the academic community by publishing journals in International Research Journal of Computer Science (IRJCS) on Artificial Intelligence Projects. In 2018, Rahul's startup was recognized as one among the top 10 startups at Global Ayurveda Summit highlighting their innovative solutions. Further recognition came from AIC IIITH, Hyderabad where the startup was awarded the Best Social Impact Startup during the Kerala Pitch Fest.
